ImmunoBlitz

Play ImmunoBlitz – The Immune System Game

ImmunoBlitz is a free, browser-based game that turns the human immune system into a fast-paced defense challenge. Built by BrainStorm Games, it helps students and curious players understand how barriers, cells and organs work together to keep us healthy—while having fun.

How to Play

  • First choose the difficulty, then press Play to start.
  • Defend the body by popping yellow bubbles to do damage and green bubbles to heal organs.
  • Each bubble gives you Immunopoints that help you upgrade organs (e.g., skin, eye, lungs) and deploying immune responses.
  • Each wave introduces different microbes; read the brief and pick the best counters.
  • Make use of power-ups to help you in specific waves.
  • Do not pop the red bubbles since they have negative effects.
  • Try to survive as many waves as you can!

What You’ll Learn

  • The roles of the organs in your immune system.
  • How physical barriers, mucus and cilia help trap pathogens.
  • Why white blood cells (like macrophages and lymphocytes) matter.
  • Real examples of microbes and how the body responds to them.

Who Is It For?

ImmunoBlitz is designed for classrooms, families and anyone aged 8+ who wants a friendly introduction to biology. It runs on desktop browser with no download needed.
