About ImmunoBlitz

ImmunoBlitz is a free science-based game created by BrainStorm Games to make learning about the immune system fun and interactive.

As you play, you’ll encounter different immune system organs, defenses, and challenges. The goal is to help players of all ages understand immunity while having fun.
